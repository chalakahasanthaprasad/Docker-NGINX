Top Search Engine

Published, May 27, 2024

Google

Google is generally considered the world’s best search engine. It has maintained over 88% market share since 2009 and remains the most popular search engine as of June 2023. Google’s vast algorithms provide fast and accurate search results, and it offers additional services like Google Maps, YouTube, and Gmail1



Bing

Created by Microsoft in 2009, Bing is the second-largest search engine globally. Although it holds only 2.77% market share, Bing stands out for its multimedia search capabilities, including advanced image and video search results. It also introduced an AI-powered search assistant in its results
